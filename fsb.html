Flashcard Study Buddy
Flashcard Study Buddy is a voice-interactive learning tool created by me and my friend Frank Poon. It provides a convenient conversational form of studying by verbally testing users on Quizlet flashcards.

"Talk to Flashcard Study Buddy"

The application is Google approved and deployed to all Google Assistant platforms, even Google Home! Try it out with the invocation line above.
In Action


Invocate FSB and ask it to test you on a set by providing a Quizlet username and the desired set name in the format "SetName by Username"


For difficult Quizlet usernames (like mine), you can just spell the letters out and FSB will find it.


Enjoy learning with your new study buddy!
